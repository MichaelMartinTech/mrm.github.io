About

Ph.D Student and Graduate Student Researcher at Visualization & Interface Design Innovation (VIDi) Lab at the University of California, Davis, advised by Dr. Kwan-Liu Ma. My research focuses on the intersection of computer graphics, virtual reality (VR) / interactive software, Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning, explainable AI, and sound design, focusing on interactive systems that combine immersion, analysis, and insights.

I also explore interdisciplinary design in interactive media, scientific visualization, and real-time simulations - creating systems that cover art, science, and engineering.
