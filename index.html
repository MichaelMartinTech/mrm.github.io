About

Michael Martin is a Ph.D Student and Graduate Student Researcher at Visualization & Interface Design Innovation (VIDi) Lab at the University of California, Davis, advised by Dr. Kwan-Liu Ma. His expertise spans the integration of computer graphics, virtual and augmented reality (VR/AR), interactive software, artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), explainable AI (XAI), and scientific visualization.

He has designed and developed intelligent, user-centered systems that integrated immersion, analysis, and insights bridging art, science, and engineering. His research created interdisciplinary tools for broad scientific and engineering applications, real-time simulation, and interactive media that advanced scientific understanding, creativity, and human experience in complex digital environments.
